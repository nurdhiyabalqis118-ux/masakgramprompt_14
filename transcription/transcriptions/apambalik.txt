
Okay, so some time ago, I made this appam balik pizza that I'm still kind of obsessed with.
So I made it again, but this time I made it bougie.
The batter is the same as before.
I just mixed flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, yeast, and egg, and warm milk,
and left it for 30 minutes for the yeast to develop.
Then I spread the batter onto a pan and let it cook.
I put tomato and mozzarella in.
But then instead of the classic pepperoni like I did last time,
I topped this one with prosciutto, arugula, olive oil,
and some fancy homemade cured egg yolks and black pepper.
I folded it in half, and then I ate the whole thing.
Cheese, pool, and all.
It's so, so good!
