I made an absolutely diabolical dessert recently and I kind of like it. Yes, it's a pickle shaved ice.
Hear me out. I got the idea from this viral video that seemed to trigger a bunch of people,
but instead of feeling disgusted by it, it actually got me thinking if I could turn it
into an actually good dessert. In my head, the herby tanginess of the pickles could work
pretty well if it's balanced with some rich sweetness, so I tried it with some simple berries,
sweet cream, and caramel, and it was pretty good. But this doesn't seem diabolical enough
because you can just push the shaved ice aside and eat what you like, so I figured out a better
way to serve it with everything buried underneath a mound of pickle shaved ice. So you'll really
have to dig deep through your initial fear and disgust before you get to the good stuff,
and hopefully its flavor will surprise you. Not as bad as I thought. So would you try this?
