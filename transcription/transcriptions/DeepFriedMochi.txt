I made these deep fried mochi or tong yuen last week that you all love this so much, so I made another quirky dessert with it.
This is the ultimate black sesame tong yuen cookie.
Whoa, whoa! I started the cookie dough based by creaming up butter and sugar at an egg vanilla flour, baking butter and salt.
Then I folded in black sesame seeds and black sesame paste to give it this nice marble effect.
I bought up my favorite Starbucks black sesame tong yuen for five minutes until they float.
Show them on a tray, then nestle them into the cookie dough.
I baked them off and they turn out so beautifully.
I love this marble effect and just look at this cross section. Oh my god, it's crumbly in crisp and
outside and truly chewy and gooey on the inside. Okay, I think I'm obsessed with how
versatile these tong yuens are. So, what should I make with them next?
