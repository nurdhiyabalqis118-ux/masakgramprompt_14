This is the most perfect pancake I've ever made. Now before you say it's AI generated, it's not.
Some of you might recognize it as the famous golden diner or pavilion style pancakes.
And yeah, since flight prices are sky high at the moment, instead of flying across the world
to try it, I made it myself. This starts off with egg whites and sugar whipped till fluffy,
then I fold it in corn flour, egg yolks, more flour, salt, and a splash of milk to create
a super airy batter. I nudge the batter into my trusty non-stick pan, then covered it to
let the bottom brown while gently cooking the top. I then held my breath as I flipped over the pancake
to cook the other side. Then I made a honey butter glaze with a bit of soy sauce and vanilla
in it too, and I plated everything up. Oh my god, just look at this. It's so voluminous
and fluffy and soft. It's unreal, I truly think.
