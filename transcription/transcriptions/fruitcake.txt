Now this might be an unpopular opinion, but I've never been a huge fan of a fruit cake
So I recently made one to suit my own palate and I think I love it because it's packed with dried Asian fruits
I took some dried persimmons and sweet potatoes, chopped them up and added goji berries, longans, red dates and raisins and soaked them
All in rum overnight. For the batter, I creamed up butter and gula melaka, added eggs, orange zest and juice and chopped up bits of Korean pear
Then in goes the flour, baking powder, Christmas spices and salt, along with all the rummy fruits before it's baked
Making this made me feel pretty festive. So let me put on my mandatory Christmas jumper
Ah, that's more like it. Now the fruit cake is very good fresh
But I also wrapped one in cheesecloth and soaked it in rum to age it
