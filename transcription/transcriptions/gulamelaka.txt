This here is my current favorite cake.
It uses gula melaka, which is a smoky, toffee-like palm sugar from Malaysia, and it's so great in desserts.
For this, we'll just melt it and add coconut milk, then make a cake batter by creaming up some butter and sugar,
then add eggs, gula melaka, and your dry ingredients.
Finally, add in the rum soaked longans, give it a mix, pour it out, and bake.
I mean, it's for Mother's Day, so I shared it with my mom, but really, every day is a good day for cake.
