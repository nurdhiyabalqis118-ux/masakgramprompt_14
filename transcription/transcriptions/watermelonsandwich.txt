This ridiculous watermelon sandwich has been getting pretty popular lately.
I thought it was a silly idea at first, but my mom always said to give everything a try at least once,
so I went and got myself a watermelon, cut out two squarish pieces,
then I lay it on the tomatoes and mozzarella, some basil leaves,
and drizzled it with olive oil, balsamic vinegar, flaky salt and pepper.
Oh, I also added a bit of prosciutto because I like the flavors together.
I then bit into this and it was surprisingly good!
I thought this would be a mess to eat to be honest, but it held up pretty well
and the flavors just classically go well together.
Although I have to say, it is very watermelon-forward.
But still, overall, I think it's a good one!
