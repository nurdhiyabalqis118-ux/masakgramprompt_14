This is the classic Malaysian drink of air mata kucing.
And this is the same drink elevated and disguised as an espresso martini.
Here's how I made it.
The classic air mata kucing uses dried longan, lo-hon-gor or manfurt, pandan, and gula melaka.
So I got them all and simmered them together until it's dark, broody, and intense.
Then I added in espresso.
In fact, you don't even need an espresso machine since you can use a shot of the new Nescafe espresso Concentrate.
The sweet vanilla one, especially lends so well to this drink.
So just pour it, mix it, and hack it in your own way, like I did.
To end up with a fun, flavorful drink that plays with your expectations.
In fact, it's so fun that I'm going to give it a fun name.
So I'm calling it my espresso mart-a kucing!.
