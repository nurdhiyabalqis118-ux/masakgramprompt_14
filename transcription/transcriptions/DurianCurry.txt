This is a durian. Some say it's a smelliest fruit in the world, but to me that means it's
a culinary challenge. Since it has a strong and sweet flavor, it's usually eaten on
its own or in desserts, so I tried working it into a savory dish, which is this durian curry.
I started with a classic Malaysian chicken curry base, okay I cheated and used some store bought curry paste here
but then I added chicken, some cut up potatoes and carrots, and
let this simmer for half an. Then when it's done, I put some durian in, okay not like that.
I smooshed up the flesh and gently temper it into the curry, and this tasted weirdly good I
guess. Durian fans would love it for sure, but my favorite thing about it is the texture.
The durian turns the curry thick and gloopy in the best way. Try it.
