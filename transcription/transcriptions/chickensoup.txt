Have you heard about this chicken soup that's made without a single drop of water?
People have been going crazy about this waterless chicken soup lately,
so I wanted to try it for myself.
It starts off with a bunch of vegetables, an onion, an apple, a carrot, some napa cabbage,
all thrown into a big pot together with a whole chicken.
I also added spring onions, ginger and goji berries to mine.
I then cooked this over medium-low heat for an hour,
and over time, it did release a surprising amount of juice.
This liquid gold is dripping with pure umami savouriness from the chicken
and a mellow, satisfying sweetness from the apples and carrots.
Having this with a bowl of rice was super comforting
and nourished the deepest corners of my heart.
So if you're in need of some heart nourishment, here's your sign to make this.
