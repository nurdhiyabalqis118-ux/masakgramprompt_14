This is kuih talam, XL Kopi Kau Kau Extra Intense Melele.
Okay sorry, maybe I went too hard.
While the classic kui talam is all about coconut and pandan,
I made a modern take on it with a beautifully rich coconut and coffee custard layer.
So first, for the base, I used the new Nescafe Gold Intenso,
which is an intensely dark roasted instant coffee
that is bold and vibrant and works so well in this dessert.
I dissolved it in water together with gula melaka,
then added in a bunch of flour mixed with water,
poured it into a mold and steamed it until it sets.
Then I made my coconut milk layer with rice and tapioca flour,
water, sugar and salt.
I poured it on top, steamed it again and sliced it up.
Oh my god, just look at how soft and jelly-like it is.
It's tender, super fragrant,
and intensely flavorful with that coconut and coffee combo.
Man, kui and kopi, it's so dreamy.
