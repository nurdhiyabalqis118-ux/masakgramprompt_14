These peanut cookies are my weakness every Lunar New Year, and recently it's especially bad because I found a super quick and easy way to make them on my own with peanut butter!
While the traditional peanut cookies use peanuts that you'll have to roast and ground,
I just straight up used peanut butter, mixed it with all-purpose flour, sugar, salt,
baking powder, oil, and a dash of sesame oil for extra fragrance. 
I stirred this all together, then rolled it into little balls, decorated each one with a peanut on top, and baked them for 18 minutes. 
And yeah, these will turn out just as good if not even better than those peanut cookies that you get in stores, except it has that extra homemade aroma and
tastiness to it, which is so addictive. 
Try it!
