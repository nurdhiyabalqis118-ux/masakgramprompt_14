I made these mad coconut-y, Thai milk-y ice cream bars, and it was quite the accident.
I initially set out to make a Thai tea semi-freddo, so I made a sabayon by heating up eggs and
sugar and whisking it until ribbony.
I then infused some heavy cream with Thai tea leaves before chilling it and whipping
it up.
I folded the two together and froze it in a loaf tin, but when I unmolded it I realised
I forgot to line the tin so I had to melt the sides and it got a bit ugly.
I ended up just cutting it into blocks, then coated the sides with toasted coconut flakes
to cover the uglies, and froze it again.
And in the end, they actually turned out into these really nice, toasty ice cream
snacks that taste of creamy coconut-y tea.
