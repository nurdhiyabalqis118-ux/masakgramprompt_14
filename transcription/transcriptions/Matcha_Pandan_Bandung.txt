This is bandung. 
It's a classic Malaysian drink made with rose syrup and evaporated milk, and I'm sorry to all Malaysians but I don't really like it.
I often find it too sweet and syrupy, but since it's Ramadan, I thought to make a bandung-inspired drink.
So I kept the base the same with rose syrup and evaporated milk, but to cut through that saccharine sweetness, I made pandan juice from some fresh pandan leaves, and whisk it together with matcha powder until it's foamy.
I then topped the bandung base with ice, fresh milk and the pandan matcha mix, and oh this was surprisingly much much better than I expected.
Gone is the simple sweetness of the rose syrup,
because here you get the light fragrance of the rose, but taste-wise there's a nice earthy, bitter sweetness that is so balanced, I finished the whole thing.
Try it out!
