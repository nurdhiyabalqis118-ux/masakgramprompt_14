Recently, I've been having cravings for a classic Malaysian chicken rice, but since it's
a national month, I'm also feeling like having a nasi lemak, which is Malaysian national dish.
So, I combine the two into this Frankenstienian dish of chicken lemak rice.
For this, I cook the chicken that classically, gently simmer in a light bath with ginger,
spring onion, and salt. But for the lemak part, I also added an instant coconut milk.
Then, I took the chicken out, chilled up the stock, and then used some of the chicken-y coconut-y
liquid to cook the rice. Oh, and I also blend in a quick chili sauce, because chicken rice
wouldn't be complete without it. And then, it's done! This tastes like a chicken rice with a richer
fragrance of the coconut milk. And while some people might say I put a two famous dishes here,
I was very full and satisfied at the end. So, all is well.
