This dessert is inspired by a deliriously good corn tart I had at a cafe when I went to Seoul
some weeks ago. It was sweet, savory, and full of corn flavour, and I loved it so much that I made
this. The corn here comes in three ways. There's some cream corn, sauteed brown butter corn,
and also the corniest, most important part, corn custard. To make this, I simmered some corn
and milk, including the cobs for extra flavour, before blending and whisking it into egg yolks
and sugar, then adding flour, cornstarch, and a touch of vanilla and rum, and finally folding
in whipped cream. Oh, there was also some crispy oats that I made by toasting them in the oven.
Then I just rolled out the tart shell, baked it, and layered everything together
to get this super corny dessert. And it tasted pretty amazing.
