This is a banana creme brule. It's an easy dessert that I think is also kind of fun and
inventive, so let's make it. First I got a banana sliced up the top like this and gently
teased out the flesh. I cut up the banana, saut ed it in the pan until soft, then added milk and
blended it up. I then poured in whipping cream, vanilla, cinnamon powder, egg yolks and sugar,
and heated it up until it's a thick banana custard. I let this chill in the fridge,
then poured it into the banana peel. I sprinkled sugar on top, torched it to give it that
crackly caramel top, and then I ate the whole thing. This was truly so good and rich with
banana flavour, but best of all, I love that there's barely any cleaning up after. Try it!
