Since you all love my roti-boy toast recipe so much, here's my next invention,
roti-boy cookies. Hear me out, it has that crackly coffee crust and yes,
a whole hunk of butter inside, but now in cookie form. This starts with the cookie dough base.
I cleaned up butter and sugar, added instant coffee, egg, vanilla, flour, baking powder,
and salt. I chilled this, divided it, and wrapped a piece of butter inside each dough.
I then made a coffee paste with instant coffee mixed with butter, sugar, and flour.
I flattened out each cookie and spread the coffee paste on top so they end up like this
after baking. I gave it a rain of flaky salt and I got these beautiful cookies that are
just as fragrant and flavorful as any roti-boy bun. And best of all,
my kitchen was perfumed like a coffee bun bakery. Try it!
