This papaya snow fungus soup is what I'll make when anyone in my family falls sick.
Every Chinese household probably has their own version of this, but mine starts off with
snow fungus, red dates, goji berries and dried longans all soaked in water then put into a pot
to simmer. I sweeten it with rock sugar then let it cook for an hour. Hey, it rhymes! Anyway,
I then added chunks of papaya to cook for another 30 minutes. And while my mom never
added papaya in hers, I really really like the sugar sweetness it adds. So you'll end up
with a dessert soup that feels like a warm, nourishing hug that is especially comforting
when you're down with the flu. So the next time you're feeling under the weather, give this a try!
