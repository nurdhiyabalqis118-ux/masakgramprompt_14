Is chicken rice better when they're balls? This is a Hainanese chicken rice dish made famous in
the state of Malacca in Malaysia and I love chicken rice so I had to try it. First I cook
the chicken, this goes into a pot of water together with ginger, scallion and salt and
pepper. I cooked this for half an hour then took the chicken out. Next for the rice,
I sauteed some garlic and ginger in butter, poured in the rice and cooked it with the
chicken broth which makes it 10 times more flavorful than regular rice. After it's done,
I rolled it into balls with an ice cream scoop like I learned from this inspiring video and of
course no chicken rice would be complete without the condiments so I blended together a classic
garlicky limey chili sauce and I also made a ginger scallion sauce complete with an oil sizzle.
Then I served everything up on a plate and can I just say I love chicken rice so much
I think it might be my favorite Asian dish and the balls kind of made it more fun to eat.
