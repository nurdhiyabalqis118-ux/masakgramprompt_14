Since you all love my potato noodle recipe so much, I took those wormy, squirmy noodles,
and turned them into a carbonara. It's creamy, cheesy, and so slurpable. I really like it.
This starts off just like any classic carbonara, by rendering some bacon or hot guanciale,
then I made a paste with an egg, egg yolk, too much parmesan cheese and black pepper.
Then, for the star of the show, the noodles!, I got two potatoes, cut boiled and mashed them,
then added potato starch and water to turn it into this pudgy dough. I rolled it up into these long thick noodles
that I boiled for two minutes until they float, then I put the noodles in the pan with all that bacon fat, turned off the heat and added in the cheesy sauce. It turned into the savory, slippery,
super slurpable plate of noodles and come on, Who can say no to this cute and pudgy carbonara noodles?
