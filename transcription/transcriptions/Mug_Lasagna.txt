Lasagna is one of those things that you would usually make for a crowd, but there's no
shame in making a lasagna for one, right?
Even if there was, I did make a solo lasagna recently, in a mug.
I started the mug-sagna by cooking my ground beef with onions and kimchi for extra flavour, then I got my favourite mug, which I wasn't sure was entirely oven safe, so I just prayed that it wouldn't crack.
I then constructed my lasagna layers with tomato sauce, meat, some broken up pasta
pieces, ricotta and mozzarella cheese.
I did like 3 levels here, ending with extra cheese on top, then I baked this in the oven and it got nicely caramelised and really gooey.
It was just as good as any lasagna, only a lot easier to make.
So you see, some recipes really do work better when it's made for one.
